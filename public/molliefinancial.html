Mollie Financial LMS


The goal of this project was to propose a future extensive and feasible system design to Mollie Financial's LMS (Lease Management System). I used design methodologies and conducted various forms of user research and usability testing to ensure that this product would meet customer/company needs.
Role: UI/UX Designer
Tools: Sketch, Photoshop, Illustrator, After Effects, Invision

Beginning

As my first side project at UW, we decided to design a service that applies to us. Right before the meeting, we could not decide where should we meet for the meeting, tired of switching between the messaging and the map application. Let's build a map-based messaging service for geospatial information & context sharing and decision making!
